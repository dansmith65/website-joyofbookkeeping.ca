Mission Statement

To provide a positive bookkeeping experience, and free up people to focus on their business.

Services offered

  • Enter invoices and payments (accounts receivable)
  • Enter expenses and pay bills (accounts payable)
  • Bank statement reconciliations
  • Credit card reconciliations
  • Payroll remittance
  • GST/PST remittance
  • Worksafe remittance
  • T4’s and ROE
  • Month End Statements
  • Financial statements

We are committed to providing accurate, timely, and confidential services.

We require a retainer for all historical bookkeeping. We have found it to be beneficial for all parties, to charge an hourly rate instead of a flat monthly fee. Please let us know if you would like an estimate on services.
